## Retrieval routes

- **UiO Oria** (oria.no): institutional access to Wiley, Elsevier, Sage, Taylor & Francis
- **Annual Reviews**: UiO has full access — use for the annurev paper
- **PNAS / Nature**: both available via UiO
- **Royal Society Open Science**: gold OA — should be directly accessible; retry PDF download
- **Unpaywall browser extension**: try the DOI link directly in browser
- **Author pages / ResearchGate**: for papers not available institutionally
