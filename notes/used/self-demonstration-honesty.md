# Note: Self-demonstration — honest account of how this paper was actually written

**Source:** Author reflection (2026-04-19)

---

## The honest account

The paper's intended demonstration was a systematic AI workflow applied throughout. The
reality is more complicated, and I need to be honest about this.

My thinking developed considerably during the work. The reviewer skills and enquiries
into ideas that came up along the way — Frankfurt, Gelman, the pre-registration spectrum,
the review protocol, the persona design — were productive precisely because they were not
all pre-specified. Some of the most important conceptual moves in the paper emerged during
the process rather than being planned before it. The logging system probably shows this;
anyone reading the full session record would see that the paper developed hermeneutically
as much as systematically.

Not all stages were as structured as the paper implies they were. The systematic aspect
lies under it all — the session logging, the reviewer skills, the author-input files, the
project configuration — but the paper's actual development was messier, more iterative,
and more exploratory than the presentation suggests.

I am not surprised by this. The most rigorous empirical social science research is often
like this in reality too. The question is not whether the process was perfectly systematic
from the start, but whether the systematic elements did real epistemic work when they were
applied.

---

## Why this does not undermine the paper — and why it strengthens a specific argument

This is not a confession that damages the paper. It is actually the honest demonstration
the paper needs to make.

The paper argues for a spectrum of systematic AI use, not a binary. This paper places
itself on that spectrum — closer to the systematic end in some respects (session logs,
reviewer skills, author-input files, project configuration, literature search procedure),
more fluid in others (conceptual development, argument elaboration, the hermeneutic
movement between ideas). That is exactly what the spectrum argument predicts: the
systematic and the exploratory are not opposites. The systematic infrastructure supported
the exploratory development; it did not prevent it.

The philosopher persona's concern — that writing is thinking, that the process
constitutes the argument rather than merely expressing it — applies to this paper. The
paper's argument developed through the writing. The reviewer skill sessions and the
enquiries into ideas that emerged along the way were a form of writing-as-thinking through
AI: using the AI to surface implications, press on weak points, and extend arguments in
directions that were not initially specified. This is closer to the hermeneutic end of the
spectrum than to the pre-registration end.

This is honest and important. It means the paper's self-demonstration is not of a perfectly
rigid systematic workflow, but of what the spectrum actually looks like in practice at a
point that is systematic-but-not-rigid. That is a stronger demonstration than a
perfectly pre-specified workflow would have been, because it is more representative of how
serious research actually proceeds.

---

## What the paper should say

The paper should acknowledge — probably in §5.4 or the scope section — that its own
composition places it on the spectrum rather than at the rigid end of it. The systematic
elements were real: the session logs are contemporaneous, the author-input files document
intellectual origin, the reviewer skills provided genuine external-facing critique, the
literature search followed a documented procedure. But the argument developed during the
work, and that development was not always pre-specified.

The honest formulation is something like: this paper demonstrates systematic AI use in
the sense that the infrastructure was in place and used throughout, and the systematic
elements did real epistemic work; it does not demonstrate perfectly pre-registered,
outcome-blind systematic use, and does not claim to. The paper places itself where most
serious research that uses this approach will place itself — on the spectrum, not at
either extreme.

This also responds directly to the philosopher's critique and to the pre-registration
critique. The paper is not self-undermining on the writing-as-thinking point; it is an
instance of writing-as-thinking through AI, conducted within a systematic infrastructure
that made the thinking visible and recoverable. That is precisely the claim the paper makes
for the framework.

---

## Implications for the limitations discussion

The most rigorous research in social science is often more hermeneutic than it appears in
the published account. The systematic elements in this paper are real, but so is the
developmental character of the work. The logging system captures this, which is itself a
demonstration of the framework's transparency function: a reader with access to the
project record can see how the argument developed, which is more than most published
methodological papers offer.

The limitation to acknowledge is not that the paper was unsystematic. It is that the
self-demonstration it offers is of a moderately systematic, hermeneutically developed
workflow — not of a maximally systematic, pre-registered one. That is an honest limitation
and one that makes the paper more, not less, useful to readers who will find themselves
in similar positions.
